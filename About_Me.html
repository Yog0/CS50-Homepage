About Me

I'm a mechatronics engineer from Melbourne, Australia. After studying music, working as a martial arts instructor and doing a whole lot of other interesting things on the side, I decided to go back to uni and learn to make things. I've worked on some really interesting projects, including building the electrical system for a Formula SAE car.

Outside of work, I love to learn new things and have been working my way through CS50 and web development courses. I train Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu regularly - it's the most fun way I know to keep fit as well as being really challenging and letting me meet interesting new people. I make electronic music which you can find here and used to make jazz, which you can find here.
